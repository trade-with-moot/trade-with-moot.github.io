trade-with-moot Privacy Policy

Effective 31 May 2026

This policy explains how trade‑with‑moot (“trade-with-moot,” “we,” “us”) handles information in connection with its website, dashboard, and applications (the “Service”).

The short version: trade‑with‑moot publishes its own content to its own social accounts. We do not collect, sell, or share personal information about visitors or other users. Our apps do not request access to your accounts or data.

1. Information we do not collect

The website is a static informational site. We do not run accounts, logins, trackers, advertising pixels, or analytics that identify you. We do not ask visitors for personal information, and we do not sell or rent any data to anyone.

2. Our publishing applications

We operate software that publishes the project’s own daily content to platforms we control — for example, TikTok via the TikTok Content Posting API. In that flow:

  • The app authenticates only to our own trade‑with‑moot accounts to post our own videos and captions.
  • Access tokens for those accounts are stored privately by the project operator and are never published or shared.
  • The app does not request, read, or store the personal data, content, or followers of any other user.

3. Server logs

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process standard, non-identifying server request logs (such as IP address and user agent) as a data processor under its own privacy policy, to deliver and secure the site. We do not access those logs to identify individuals.

4. Third-party platforms

Links to and integrations with third parties (TikTok, Instagram, GitHub, Streamlit, and others) are governed by those parties’ own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Children

The Service is general-audience content and is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

6. Your requests & changes

Because we don’t collect personal information about visitors, there is generally nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you believe we hold information about you, contact us and we will respond. We may update this policy; the effective date above reflects the latest revision.

7. Contact

Privacy questions: liyingjie@journey-ai.net.
