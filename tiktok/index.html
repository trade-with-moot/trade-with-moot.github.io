How we use TikTok 🍵

Our app publishes our own daily recap videos to our own @trade_with_moot account — nothing more.

trade‑with‑moot runs a public, 3‑year AI‑trading experiment. Each U.S. market close, our software renders one short 9:16 “Daily Close” recap and posts it to our social accounts. On TikTok, it does this through TikTok’s official Login Kit and Content Posting API.

The publisher (in‑app)

Our TikTok Publisher console — connect the account, pick the day’s video, publish to TikTok.

What happens, step by step

  1. Connect with TikTok (Login Kit)The operator authorizes our own @trade_with_moot account on TikTok’s consent screen. The browser returns to our verified domain (trade-with-moot.github.io/tiktok/callback/).
  2. Pick the day’s videoThe app selects the freshly rendered Daily Close recap (a 9:16 MP4 we produced).
  3. Publish via the Content Posting APIThe app initializes an upload, sends the video file, and delivers it to the account — either straight to the profile (Direct Post) or to the creator inbox for a one‑tap publish.
  4. It appears on @trade_with_mootThe video is live on our own profile, posted entirely by our own integration.

Permissions we request

user.info.basicvideo.uploadvideo.publish

user.info.basic confirms which account we’re connected to; video.upload and video.publish let the app upload and post our own recap videos to our own account.

First-party only. The app authenticates solely to accounts we own and posts solely our own content. It never reads, stores, or posts on behalf of any other TikTok user, and it requests no data beyond the account it is connected to.

The wider project

Every position weight, rebalance, and the full equity curve are public on our live dashboard, judged against the market over 1,095 days. It is entertainment and education — not financial advice.
