trade‑with‑moot 🍵

An AI trading a real money account — in public, every day, for three years.

trade‑with‑moot is an experiment in radical transparency. Every position weight, every rebalance, and the full equity curve are published openly and judged against the market over 1,095 days (2026–2029). No paid signals, no “buy this now” — just one AI’s decisions, shown in the open.

What it is

Moot is an automated (“AI”) trading agent. It reads market data, weighs a blend of signals, sizes positions under fixed risk limits, and places real orders in a brokerage account — with no human picking the trades. Everything it does is logged and published the same day, win or lose. The point isn’t to win; it’s to answer one question honestly, in the open: can an AI actually make money in the market over the long run?

Entertainment & education, not financial advice. Nothing here is a recommendation to buy or sell any security. Markets carry risk, including loss of principal. Do your own research.

How the experiment works

Real account

Moot trades a funded brokerage account — not a simulation. Results are the real thing.

Signals & risk gates

It blends several measurable signals into one score, then hard risk limits (caps per name, per theme, and stops) decide what’s allowed.

Public receipts

Each market close, the day’s result, holdings weights, and equity curve are posted to the live dashboard and social accounts.

Three-year clock

The experiment runs 1,095 days regardless of outcome. A losing stretch is on-thesis content, not something to hide.

The app & how we publish

trade‑with‑moot operates its own software to publish the project’s daily content to the accounts we control. For TikTok, the application uses TikTok’s Login Kit and Content Posting API to post our own videos and captions to our own @trade_with_moot account. The app authenticates only to our accounts; it never reads, stores, or posts on behalf of any other user.

FAQ

Is this financial advice?

No. It’s a public experiment and entertainment. It is not investment, financial, legal, or tax advice, and not a recommendation to buy or sell anything.

Do you sell signals or take money to recommend trades?

Never. There are no paid signals, no “DM for calls,” and nothing for sale that influences what Moot does.

Can I copy the trades?

You can watch everything Moot does, but you shouldn’t treat it as advice. What works for an experiment may be wrong for you.

How long does it run?

1,095 days — three years — from 2026 to 2029, with the full track record kept public throughout.
